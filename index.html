My Career

My name is Harshil Rao, and I am currently pursuing my education at Conestoga College Milton Campus.

With a strong background in photography and videography, I have accumulated 4 years of experience in the field. My professional journey began at Rowan University in the United States, where I worked as both a photographer and videographer. Subsequently, I ventured into entrepreneurship by starting my own photography small business in India. In addition to my personal endeavors, I have been contributing my skills part-time to Invincible NGO, a non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive impact on society.

Welcome To Photography World

Photography is the art of capturing light with a camera, usually via a digital sensor or film, to create an image. With the right camera equipment, you can even photograph wavelengths of light invisible to the human eye, including UV, infrared, and radio. The first permanent photograph was captured in 1826 (some sources say 1827) by Joseph Nicéphore Niépce in France. It shows the roof of a building lit by the sun.
